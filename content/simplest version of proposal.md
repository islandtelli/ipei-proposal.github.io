Growing the artists, organizations, and businesses of PEI’s creative sector through the development and promotion of a shared online directory.
### Overview

Currently pei.art exists as a compelling online directory highlighting PEI’s creative sector professionals, businesses, and organizations. 

Each profile on the platform includes all of the information a member needs to promote their creative practice, including biographies, artist statements, photo galleries, embedded audio and video files, business hours, location map and directions, and a contact form. 

Its initial development was made possible with funding from the Canada Council for the Arts, and Innovation PEI. 

With limited promotion, over 250 members have created media-rich profiles and some have already begun to receive business inquiries through the platform. However, without resources to invest in further improvement and marketing of the service, its growth has slowed, leaving much potential unrealized.

We propose that, with further investment, this strong foundation can quickly progress into a sophisticated, scalable and cost-effective shared marketing and e-commerce platform that will become an essential destination for directory members and visitors alike. 

### Phase 1 of 4 - Optimize Directory

*Short term - over next 6 months*

Phase 1 will see the basic directory layout of the home page and discipline-specific landing pages optimized for lead generation and sales.
